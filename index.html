Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 11, 2026

Thank you for playing Stack Dash ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide you with a great gaming experience and to maintain our global leaderboard:

  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address and a password. This is securely handled by our backend provider (Supabase) and is used solely to authenticate your identity.
  • Game Data: We collect your in-game display name, high scores, and gold coins balance. This data is synced to our database to populate the global leaderboard and save your progress across devices.

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs, and we do not collect data for targeted advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your user account.
  • Display your chosen name and score on the public global leaderboard.
  • Save your game progress (scores, and gold balance).
  • Maintain and improve the App's functionality.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share data with service providers that help us operate the App (e.g., Supabase for database hosting and authentication). These providers are bound by strict confidentiality and data protection agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encryption of data in transit. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your account data and high scores for as long as your account is active. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address provided below. Upon your request, we will promptly delete your personal data from our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for users of all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.
